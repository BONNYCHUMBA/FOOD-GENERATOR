WELCOME TO YOUR KITCHEN

Food is a fascinating subject that goes beyond mere sustenance. Did you know that honey is the only food that never spoils? Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible! Another intriguing fact is that apples float in water because they are made up of 25% air. And let's not forget the world's most expensive coffee, Kopi Luwak, which is made using beans that have been eaten and excreted by a civet cat. Food is not just about taste; it's a blend of history, science, and culture that makes every bite an adventure.

UGALI is a staple food in Kenya and is enjoyed by many communities across the country? Made from maize flour and water, this simple yet versatile dish is often served with vegetables, meat, or fish. Ugali holds a special place in Kenyan culture, symbolizing unity and tradition.
